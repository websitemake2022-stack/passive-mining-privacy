Privacy Policy for Passive Mining Rewards
Effective Date: 23 April 2026
App Package: com.passivemining.app

This Privacy Policy explains how Passive Mining Rewards (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Important: Passive Mining Rewards uses simulation-based earning features. It does not perform real cryptocurrency mining on your device.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information you provide
- Account details: username, email address, password (stored securely in hashed form).
- Profile details: username/payout profile information you enter in the app.
- Withdrawal details: wallet/payout address and withdrawal request amount/details.

B. Information collected automatically
- Device identifiers (for security/fraud prevention).
- IP address and network metadata.
- App activity and logs (login events, mining session events, ad reward events, transaction events, withdrawal status events).
- Crash/performance diagnostics.

C. Advertising-related data
When ads are enabled, ad partners (such as Google AdMob and/or Unity Ads) may collect device and advertising identifiers and related ad interaction data according to their own policies.

2. How We Use Information
We use data to:
- Create and manage accounts.
- Authenticate users and secure access.
- Run simulation earning logic and maintain wallet/transaction records.
- Process and review withdrawal requests.
- Prevent abuse, fraud, duplicate rewards, and suspicious activity.
- Operate rewarded ads and enforce reward rules.
- Improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
- Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis (where applicable)
Depending on your region, we process data based on:
- Performance of a contract (providing app features),
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement),
- Consent (where required, such as personalized ads),
- Legal compliance.

4. Sharing of Information
We do not sell personal data.
We may share data with:
- Service providers and infrastructure providers (hosting, database, monitoring).
- Advertising/mediation partners (e.g., AdMob, Unity Ads) when ads are used.
- Legal authorities when required by law or to enforce rights/safety.
- Internal admins for moderation, support, security, and withdrawal review.

5. Data Retention
We keep data only as long as needed for:
- Account operation and app features,
- Security and fraud-prevention records,
- Legal, tax, dispute, and compliance obligations.
When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized.

6. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including:
- Access controls,
- Token-based authentication,
- Password hashing,
- Encrypted transport (HTTPS where available),
- Security monitoring and abuse prevention.
No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve protections.

7. Children’s Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child submitted data, contact us for deletion.

8. International Data Transfers
Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. We take reasonable steps to protect data during such transfers.
